who we are

This 6 weeks Reality TV show (Woman on a mission) will bring together 37 women (representing the 37 states of Nigeria) in order to help them discover their purpose, their place in the community,and the power they hold to societal change and family repositioning, which is the key ingredient for transformation in the world today/

Women on a Mission encourages women in various fields of endeavor, to see themselves as ambassadors of a positive change in our society. We are here to help women become successful and achieve their dream profession.

We create a proper awareness on how women can be empowered, mostimportantly femaleyouths.
